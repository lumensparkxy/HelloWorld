Features of MacBook Pro 15 inch - 2015

Launching on 18 June with WWDC keynotes. available from 19 June

1. Comes with USB type-C connector.

2. Retina display, Screen resolution remains same

3. New Force-touch trackpad

4 Octa-core i5/i7 processor
5. 16Gb RAM
6. Next generation faster SSD. Starts with 256Gb, Supports until 2TB
7. Upgraded WiFi (600 Mbps)
8. Swift programming language is pre-installed
9. Comes in white, black and golden color
10. 10hrs battery
11. Thinner by 25-33%
12. lighter by 20%
13. Priced between $1999 to $2199 for basic model
14. Serious threat for PC market (best in class)
